Got Questions?

NEED TO KNOW

Do you deliver across Lagos? add
Yes! We currently deliver across Lagos Island, Victoria Island, Lekki (Phase 1 & 2), Ikoyi, Ajah, Lagos Mainland, Surulere, Yaba, and Ikeja. We're expanding rapidly — enter your address at checkout to confirm coverage.
How do you package liquid soups & stews? add
All soups — including Egusi, Pepper Soup, and Stews — are sealed in leak-proof, double-walled containers with heat-lock lids. They're placed upright in insulated bags to maintain temperature and prevent spills during transit.
What are your estimated delivery times? add
Standard delivery is typically 30–45 minutes for nearby areas (VI, Lekki Phase 1, Ikoyi). Mainland deliveries typically run 45–60 minutes. Peak hours (12pm–2pm, 7pm–9pm) may add 15–20 minutes. We'll notify you in real time.
Can I customise spice levels? add
Absolutely. Use the "Special Instructions" field at checkout. We can adjust spice levels for most dishes. Note: some dishes like Pepper Soup and Suya have a minimum heat level to preserve their authentic character.
Do you cater for large events or group orders? add
Yes — we offer party packs and event catering for groups of 20+. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance via our Catering Enquiry form. Special rates apply for bulk orders of Jollof Rice, Suya, and selected British platters.
Are there vegetarian or vegan options? add
Several dishes can be made vegetarian on request — including our Ofada Rice (served without meat), Egusi Soup (with tofu), and most British sides. A dedicated vegan menu is coming soon. Look for the 🌿 tag in upcoming menu updates.

Every ingredient is sourced at peak season. Every flame is precisely controlled. Every plate travels to you in temperature-sealed packaging engineered to preserve that elusive char.
