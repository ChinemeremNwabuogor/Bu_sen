Our Philosophy

Cooked with Fire,
Delivered with Care

EMBER was born from a singular obsession: to make fire-kissed, restaurant-quality food accessible beyond the dining room. Our brigade of chefs works with live-fire hearths, Josper ovens, and decades of craft.

Every ingredient is sourced at peak season. Every flame is precisely controlled. Every plate travels to you in temperature-sealed packaging engineered to preserve that elusive char.
